# 📖 THE COMPILE CODEX

Welcome to the **Compile Codex**. In this living document, you will find Rules Explanations, Frequently Asked Questions, and Clarifications, Rulings, and Errata for cards released for Compile.

---

## 📋 General Rules Overview

### The Turn Cycle
1.  **Start Phase:** Persistent effects and "Turn Start" triggers activate.
2.  **Check Compile Phase:** The active player checks if they meet compilation requirements (Value ≥ 10 AND > opponent) in any line. Max 1 compilation per turn.
3.  **Action Phase:** The player may either play 1 card (Face-up if matching protocol, or Face-down) OR "Refresh" (draw until they have 5 cards).
4.  **Check Cache Phase:** If the player has more than 5 cards, they must discard down to 5.
5.  **End Phase:** "Turn End" triggers activate.

### Stack Resolution (LIFO)
When multiple effects are triggered (e.g., a card effect allows playing another card which then triggers its own effect), Compile uses a **Last-In, First-Out (LIFO)** system. The most recently triggered effect is resolved completely before returning to the previous one.

## ⚖️ Clarifications, Rulings & Errata

### General Errata
*   **Face-down Cards:** All cards played face-down have a base Value of **2**, regardless of the value printed on their face-up side.

### Protocol Specific Errata (10/2024 Updates)

#### 🟣 Espíritu (Spirit)
*   **Espíritu 1:** While this card is face-up and uncovered (on top of a stack), you may play face-up cards into any line, regardless of whether the card's protocol matches the line's protocol.

#### 🔴 Muerte (Death)
*   **Muerte 1:** This card cannot be moved, flipped, or eliminated by other effects.
*   **Muerte 1 (Ability):** *Start:* You may draw 1 card. If you do, eliminate 1 other card (anywhere) and then eliminate Muerte 1.

#### 🟢 Vida (Life)
*   **Vida 0:** *Final:* If this card is covered (not the top card of its stack) at the end of your turn, eliminate it.

#### ⚔️ Odio (Hate) - Special Release
*   **Odio 2:** *Action:* Eliminate your highest value uncovered card. Then, eliminate your opponent's highest value uncovered card. 
    *   *Ruling:* If **Odio 2** is your highest value uncovered card when its effect resolves, it eliminates itself. If this happens, the second part of the effect (eliminating an opponent's card) **does not** trigger.

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can I compile in a line that my opponent has already compiled?**  
A: Yes. Compiling a line that has already been compiled by your opponent allows you to "re-compile" it. You don't get a new protocol badge, but you steal the top card of your opponent's deck as a bonus.

**Q: Does "Refresh" count as my action?**  
A: Yes. You can either play a card OR refresh. You cannot do both in the same turn unless a card effect explicitly allows it.
